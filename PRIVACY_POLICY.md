# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 24, 2026**

## What This Is About

We built LinkedIn Lead Checker to help you analyze LinkedIn profiles using AI. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## What We Collect

### Information You Give Us
- **Account Info**: Email address, password (encrypted), name
- **Payment Info**: Handled by Stripe (we never see your full card details)
- **LinkedIn Data**: Profile information you choose to analyze

### Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data**: Which features you use, how often
- **Technical Data**: Browser type, IP address, device information
- **Extension Data**: Chrome extension interactions

## How We Use Your Data

### AI Analysis
- We send LinkedIn profile data to OpenAI's API to generate insights
- OpenAI processes this data according to their own privacy policy
- We don't train AI models on your data

### Service Operations
- Deliver the analysis you requested
- Process your subscription payments
- Send you service updates and receipts
- Improve our features

### We Don't
- ❌ Sell your data to third parties
- ❌ Show you ads
- ❌ Share your data for marketing purposes
- ❌ Store LinkedIn profile data longer than necessary

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI
We use OpenAI's API for AI-powered profile analysis. Your profile data is sent to OpenAI for processing. Read their privacy policy at: https://openai.com/privacy

### Stripe
We use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe handles your payment information securely. Read their privacy policy at: https://stripe.com/privacy

### Chrome Web Store
If you use our Chrome extension, Google's privacy policies also apply.

## Data Storage & Security

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Database**: Stored securely with industry-standard protection
- **Retention**: We keep your data while your account is active
- **Location**: Data stored on secure cloud servers
